Should you host?

If you aren't sure about self-hosting or letting us host for you, here's a brief guide to help.

Are you familiar with maintaining and installing security patches for Linux? Do you know how to SSH and make your way around a unix machine without a graphical interface? If you do then you'll probably be able to host yourself. If not, we'll do that for you.

Do you have nightmares about monitoring alerts and downtime? If so, let us have those nightmares for you. If you don't mind, you're crazy and brave, but you'll be happy to self-host.

Do you value your time? The monthly hosting price starts at about the price of one developer hour. If you're happy to pay that to ensure you have a platform that can make you bags of money in return, we'd recommend a hosted version. If you're not revenue focused and have some spare time, self-hosting is your best bet.
